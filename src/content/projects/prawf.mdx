## Background

There was a discussion quite a while ago in [Teknologi Umum](https://t.me/teknologi_umum)
about brain throughput or something. Basically it was about how long does your
brain can maintain its performance. It was brought up because there was a
discussion about typing speed and how someone can get high Words per Minute at
the start but starts to slow down the longer they go.

I thought, it would be interesting to make a Pauli Test simulation app. So I did.

## Technical Details

For those who don't know, Pauli Test is a psychological test used to measure someone's consistency, endurance, speed, accuracy, and ability to do their job under pressure. It is commonly used in Indonesia as part of the job recruitment process.

This app is made using Web Component and RxJS. You can find the reason why I made this in [the project repo](https://github.com/elianiva/prawf)
